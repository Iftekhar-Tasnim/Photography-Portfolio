Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a session?

I recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for portrait sessions and 2-3 months for events like weddings. This ensures we can secure your preferred date and time.

What should I wear for my portrait session?

Choose solid colors and avoid busy patterns. Consider your location and the overall style you want to achieve. I'm happy to provide specific recommendations based on your session type.

How long does it take to receive my photos?

Basic sessions typically take 1 week, while premium packages may take up to 2 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you travel for sessions?

Yes! I'm available for travel within a 50-mile radius of NYC at no additional cost. For destinations beyond that, travel fees may apply.

Can I purchase additional photos after the session?

Absolutely! Additional photos can be purchased individually or as part of a package. I also offer photo editing services for existing photos.
